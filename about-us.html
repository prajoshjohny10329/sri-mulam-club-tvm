Welcome to Sri Mulam Club

A Renowned Recreation Club
in the Capital

Established in 1890, Sri Mulam Club is located in the picturesque Cotton Hills in Vazhuthcaud. Founded by the then Dewan Rama Rao of Travancore as the Union Club, it was subsequently re-christened after the Maharaja of Travancore and boasts having C.P. Ramaswamy Iyer, the architect of modern-day Travancore, as its first life member. Spread over more than 1.21 hectares (3 acres), this club has a health club, a kids' park, a dining hall, a licensed bar, a conference hall, an indoor shuttle court and a roof garden for hosting events. The club also has one of the largest wedding halls in Kerala called 'Kalyana Mandapam' with a maximum capacity for 1,500 people. Celebrations during the annual harvest festival of Onam and other prominent occasions as well as hosting tournaments and competitions for the members of the club are a regular feature at the club.
